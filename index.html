Rules
-Points scored at end of round-
Carte: 1 point to the player with the most cards
Denari: 1 point to the player with the most coins
Settebello: 1 point to the player with the 7 of coins
Premiera: 1 point to the player with the best hand
each player separates their highest scoring card from each suit
Scores are as follows:
7(21) 6(18) Ace(16) 5(15) 4(14) 3(13) 2(12) Face(10)
*no points are scored for ties*
Scopa: Bonus point whenever you clear the table
